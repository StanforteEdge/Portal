# STANFORTE GROUP
## Organisational Structure & Reporting Framework
Version 1.0 | January 2026

1. PURPOSE OF THIS DOCUMENT
This document explains how Stanforte Group is structured, how people report, and how decisions and priorities flow across the Group and its ventures.
It exists to:
Create clarity
Prevent overload and conflict
Enable speed without chaos
Protect people and mission
This is not a hierarchy document.
It is an operating clarity document.

2. GROUP OVERVIEW
Stanforte Group is a platform organisation that incubates, supports, and scales multiple mission‑driven ventures across health, education, inclusion, and wellbeing.
The Group provides shared services, standards, and governance, while ventures retain mission focus and execution autonomy.

6. REPORTING & AUTHORITY RULES
Rule 1: One Primary Reporting Line
Every staff member has:
One solid‑line manager (performance & KPIs)
At most one dotted‑line relationship (collaboration only)

Rule 2: Primary Role First
KPIs and performance are measured only against a person’s primary role.
Secondary duties are:
Time‑boxed
Visible
Not KPI‑critical

Rule 3: Founders Do Not Assign Day‑to‑Day Tasks
Except for the Group CEO’s strategic direction, tasking flows through:
Venture Leads
Or Group Operations
This protects clarity and capacity.

Rule 4: Standards vs Execution
Group Leads set standards
Ventures execute
Group functions do not micromanage venture work.

Rule 5: Escalation Path (No Short‑Circuiting)
Individual → Venture Lead → Group Ops → Group CEO
