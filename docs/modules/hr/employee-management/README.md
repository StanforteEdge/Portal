## System Overview

The Employee Management System serves as the core foundation for managing employee data and documents within the Stanfort Edge Portal. It provides comprehensive employee profile management, document handling, and serves as the base for other modules like Time & Attendance.

## Key Features

### 1. Personal Information Management
- Basic information (name, contact, etc.)
- Employment details
- Emergency contacts
- Disability accommodations
- Profile photos

### 2. Document Management
- Contracts and agreements
- Certifications and licenses
- Identity documents
- Educational certificates
- Document versioning

### 3. Professional Information
- Skills and qualifications
- Employment history
- Training records
- Performance reviews
- Career development

### 4. Team Management
- Team assignments
- Reporting structure
- Role management
- Department organization

## Integration Points

### 1. Current Modules
- Time & Attendance
- Leave Management
- Performance Management

### 2. Future Modules
- Training Management
- Recruitment
- Payroll
- Asset Management
