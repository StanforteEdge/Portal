# Signature Images

This folder contains signature images for PDF generation (Request Forms and Payment Vouchers).

## Required Signature Files:

### Team Lead Signatures (for Request Forms):
- `team-lead-administration.png` - Team Lead for Administration
- `team-lead-it.png` - Team Lead for IT
- `team-lead-programs.png` - Team Lead for Programs
- `team-lead-communications.png` - Team Lead for Communications
- `team-lead-operations.png` - Team Lead for Operations

### Approval Signatures (for both Request Forms and Payment Vouchers):
- `account-officer.png` - Account Officer signature
- `coo.png` - COO signature
- `ed.png` - Executive Director signature

### Additional Signatures (for Payment Vouchers only):
- `prepared-by.png` - Signature of person preparing PV
- `received-by.png` - Signature of payee (optional, can be left blank for manual signing)

**Total Files Needed:** 10 signature images

## Image Specifications:
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background
- **Recommended Size:** 200x80 pixels
- **Max File Size:** 100KB per image
- **Color:** Black or dark blue ink on transparent background

## Usage:
These signatures are automatically embedded in PDFs based on:
- **Request Forms:** Team Lead (based on selected team) + Account Officer + COO + ED (if applicable)
- **Payment Vouchers:** Account Officer + COO + ED (if applicable) + Prepared By + Received By

## Security Note:
Keep these signature files secure and restrict access to authorized personnel only.
